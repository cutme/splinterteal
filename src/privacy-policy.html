Cookies

Cookies are small data files that websites generate and ask to store on your computer. Most websites use cookies in one form or another. They may be used to:

  1. Remember settings, so that you don't need to keep re-entering them whenever you visit a new page
  2. Remember information that you give to a website, such as a list of products in your shopping basket
  3. Measure how you use the website so the site operators can make sure it meets your needs

To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit AboutCookies.org.

This site uses cookies to store information about how you use it, solely to make use of the site easier. No information is collected, no information is stored and no information is sold to third parties.
