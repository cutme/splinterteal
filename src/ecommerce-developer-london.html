Ecommerce Platforms

The Ecommerce market today is saturated with platforms, making it difficult to find the right one to suit your business needs. An Ecommerce platform should be easy to use and manage, aesthetically pleasing and have the flexibility to easily add features and functions as your business grows. Ultimately, it’s about offering the user a pleasurable and hassle-free shopping experience.

Ecommerce Development

It’s not just about what you sell, but how you sell it.

As well as having a great looking interface with a simple and hassle-free checkout, your store should be optimised for a premium shopping experience. Suggested products, related items and product filtering help customers find exactly what they’re looking for, while providing you with more opportunities for upselling.
The more user friendly your store, the more trust you’ll build.

Two of the most popular and trusted platforms are WooCommerce and Magento.

WooCommerce Shops

If you have a Wordpress site you can’t get much better than WooCommerce: a powerful plug-in extension that seamlessly integrates with Wordpress to create a stunning, yet simple to use interface.

WooCommerce is fully adaptable, allowing you to create a beautiful and bespoke online store that’s a pleasure for customers to use.

Magento Development

Loved for its flexibility and one of the most trusted platforms for customers around the globe, Magento is great for those with limited technical knowledge. The e-shop is easy to update and change thanks to a simple admin panel and multiple stores can be managed from one admin backend. Magento is also a good choice for those who want a multi-lingual website.

I can help you get the most of out of Magento’s feature-rich platform and build a fully customised responsive store that will perfectly suit your business needs. Magento can be scaled to suit any kind of business, however the process of getting exactly what you want can be complex.

From back end development to front end design of a user friendly store, I’ll keep you up-to-date with the latest extensions and themes while ensuring that everything on the platform runs smoothly.
