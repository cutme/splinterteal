Back end development is the behind-the-scenes technology and programming that makes your site run and where essential information is stored. Back end coding adds utility to everything that’s created at the front end. Think of it as a cruise ship: if the front end is the upper deck; the back end is the engine room.

CMS Development

Content Management Systems are used to create, edit, publish and archive your website’s content. They also give multiple users different permission levels to manage an entire website or a specific piece of content. A CMS makes managing your content easier. Features should include:

  • A simple administration interface that’s easy to use and that can be customised specifically for your needs
  • An easy extension for including functions
  • Simple template customisation
  • Functionality combined with user friendliness

But with literally thousands to choose from it can be confusing trying to pick the right CMS for you. I’ll choose the best CMS for your specific business then customise it to be tailor-made for your website so you can spend more time concentrating on the front end rather than worrying about the back end.

 

Wordpress Development


Wordpress is one of the most popular and best known CMS platforms used around the world and it’s easy to see why:

  • You can manage web content easily and efficiently from one platform
  • You don’t need to be tech savvy
  • Creating, editing, updating and linking are child’s play
  • Built-in, easy to use blog features
  • It’s Google friendly
  • It offers a high level of security

I offer custom Wordpress development and full project management to help you create and manage a bespoke, user friendly website using a CMS that can be tailor-made for your needs.
